# BumpSetCut Beta Test Tasks

**Welcome!** Thanks for testing BumpSetCut. Please go through these tasks in order and report anything that feels broken, confusing, or slow.

## Getting Started
1. Open the app for the first time
2. Go through the onboarding tutorial
3. Explore the home screen — does everything make sense?

## Upload a Video
4. Tap the **Upload** quick action on the home screen
5. Select a volleyball video from your camera roll
6. Wait for it to finish — did you see a progress indicator?
7. When prompted, give it a name
8. Find your video in the **Saved Games** library

## Organize Your Library
9. Create a new folder in the library
10. Move your video into that folder
11. Switch between **grid** and **list** view
12. Try searching for your video by name

## Process a Video
13. Tap the **Process** quick action from the home screen
14. Select your uploaded video
15. Wait for AI processing to complete (this takes a few minutes)
16. When done, choose where to save the results

## Watch Your Rallies
17. Find the processed video and tap to view rallies
18. Swipe through the detected rallies
19. Try long-pressing a rally to adjust the trim
20. Export a single rally to your camera roll

## Settings & Account
21. Open **Settings**
22. Sign in with Apple
23. Look through the available options — anything missing?

## Stress Tests (if you have time)
24. Upload a long video (10+ minutes)
25. Upload a non-volleyball video — what happens?
26. Lock your phone during processing — does it resume?
27. Force-quit the app and reopen — is your library intact?

---

## How to Report Issues

For each issue:
- **What you tapped / did**
- **What happened**
- **What you expected**
- **Screenshot or screen recording** (hold Power + Volume Up)

Don't worry about being too detailed — even "this felt weird" is useful.
